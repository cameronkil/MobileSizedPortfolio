About me


I've been enthusiastic about sport my entire life and found a strong passion for running at a young age. Soon followed was cycling. When I joined highschool, i become involved in their intense swimming programme whilst also still doing the other two disciplines. It wasn't till a couple months into my first year at highschool that i made the decision to join their triathlon programme...

since that decision 7 years ago I haven't regretted it one bit!


Throughout the many years of triathlon I have...

  • Completed 250+ races.
  • Raced in over 20+ locations in QLD/AUS.
  • Met thousands of people.
  • Made a few friends and a few enemies!
  • Crashed 20+ times
  • Raced at state, national and oceania level,
  • Won some races, DNF'd some races and lost a few races.

But most importantly through the blood, tears, and sweat i've had fun!
